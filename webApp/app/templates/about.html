FlixUA

Welcome FlixUA! This is our project for the First Assignment of the course WS of the University of Aveiro. This is a website that makes use of the Netflix titles dataset, which is available on Kaggle along with some addictions that we made such as the trailer URLs, the poster URLs and the rating. The website is made with Django and the database is GraphDB.
